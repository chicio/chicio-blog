## <MDXTitleWithIcon icon={<FaLightbulb className="text-shadow-lg" />}>Trivia & Fun Facts</MDXTitleWithIcon>

- Due to Mattel's reluctance to let Barbie be animated in any "unladylike" or unflattering way, she could not jump on or directly fight enemies. 
Instead, she maintains a specific walking pose and uses a magic charm bracelet to command animals and objects in the environment to perform actions for her.
